🤖 Main Project

SafePath

An autonomous home safety robot designed to help elderly people. It uses LiDAR, computer vision, and AI to detect falls and navigate around homes. I built this because my grandmother lives far from family and I wanted something that could actually keep her safe. Has a telescoping screen that goes up 4ft, a camera mast that extends to 10ft, tank treads like the Amazon Astro, and Face ID. Currently working on turning it into a real product I can sell.

FTC Robotics
CoZmicMonkeyZ — Team 32329

I'm on FTC Team 32329, CoZmicMonkeyZ, based in Frisco, Texas. We're a community team of 8 — meaning no school backs us. Everything we do is funded by our parents and donations from people who believe in us.

We finished our rookie season and learned a TON. This upcoming 2026-2027 season, we're going for Regionals. We know we can do it — we just need the resources to get there.

Being on a community team is tough because you don't get free workshop space or a teacher to help organize stuff. But honestly, it also means we've had to figure everything out ourselves, which has made us way better engineers and problem solvers.

If you want to help us out, we're raising $3,500 for next season on Zeffy. Every dollar helps and goes directly to registration fees, parts, and getting us to competitions.
